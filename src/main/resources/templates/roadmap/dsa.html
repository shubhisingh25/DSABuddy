Pro Tips

1 Consistency is Key

Practice daily, even if just 1 problem. Regular practice builds intuition.

2 Understand Patterns

Focus on problem-solving patterns rather than memorizing solutions.

3 Time Yourself

Simulate interview conditions by timing your problem-solving sessions.

4 Revise Regularly

Revisit solved problems weekly to reinforce your understanding.
