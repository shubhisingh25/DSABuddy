Pro Tips for DSA Success

Consistency is Key

Practice daily, even if just 1 problem. Regular practice builds intuition and pattern recognition.

Understand Patterns

Focus on problem-solving patterns rather than memorizing solutions. This helps with unseen problems.

Time Yourself

Simulate interview conditions by timing your problem-solving sessions (30-45 mins per problem).

Revise Regularly

Revisit solved problems weekly to reinforce your understanding and improve recall.

Write Clean Code

Practice writing production-quality code with proper variable names and comments from the start.

Mock Interviews

Do regular mock interviews to get comfortable explaining your thought process aloud.
